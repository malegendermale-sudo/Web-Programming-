Chocolate Chip Cookies

A simple and delicious cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 100g sugar
  • 100g butter
  • 1 egg
  • Chocolate chips

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix the butter and sugar together.
  3. Add the egg and stir well.
  4. Fold in the flour and chocolate chips.
  5. Shape into balls and bake for 12 minutes.
